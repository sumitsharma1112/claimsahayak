## 2. Verified rules — 9 records (CS-SCH-001…009) + synthesis table

Highlights that change or refine engine behaviour:
- **RD Protected Savings Scheme (para 13):** full-maturity-value death benefit for ₹100-denomination accounts with detailed eligibility (age 18–55 at opening, ≥2 years run, 24 default-free deposits, no early loan) and a **one-year claim limitation** — the only limitation period found anywhere in the corpus.
- **NSC/KVP continuation by up to THREE surviving nominees/heirs jointly** (paras 9(2)) — resolves OQ-15 for certificates.
- **SCSS continuation verbatim** (joint holder OR spouse-sole-nominee, spouse must meet eligibility **on the date of death**; separate-spouse-accounts must close) — closes OQ-8.
- **PPF statutory bar on continuation** + loan-interest offset against the eligible balance.
- **SSA pays the GUARDIAN** on the girl's death (scheme-specific payee) and treats the **guardian's death** as a compassionate premature-closure ground.
- **POSA survivor single-account test** (survivor with an existing single account must close the joint account).
- **MIS**: closure-only; ceiling re-test on joint holder's death.
- Death-interest rules per scheme captured verbatim (POSA/MIS to preceding month; SCSS/SSA scheme-rate-to-death + POSA after; TD/NSC/KVP slab systems; PPF to preceding month at PPF rate).

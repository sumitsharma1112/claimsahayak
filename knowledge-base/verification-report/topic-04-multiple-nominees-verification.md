## 2. Verified rules — 9 records (CS-MNM-001…009), highlights

- **Up to 4 nominees**, per-nominee percentage share and **per-nominee owner/trustee capacity** (statutory form structure); unequal shares allowed; unspecified shares = **equal by law** at payment.
- **Distribution algorithm** (fully deterministic): specified % → pro-rata redistribution of predeceased nominees' shares → equal split fallback → survivors take when co-nominees die post-depositor → last-deceased-nominee's-heirs vesting when none survive (locks onto Topic 3).
- **Payment mechanics:** single claim in duplicate; **payment to ALL surviving nominees per share** after acquittance on one sanction; per-nominee crossed cheque/account credit; no limit, 1 working day, sanction on the claim form.
- **Renunciation:** Form-14 disclaimer + KYC of the non-claiming nominee(s) — the only official route for one nominee to take another's share.
- **Minor+major mix:** majors paid directly; minor's share to depositor-appointed payee, else guardian; natural guardian needs no certificate; "minor alive" certificate; direct payment to minor void.
- **Documents/forms:** no affidavit/indemnity on the nomination track; death proof of every deceased nominee in original; ID of every claiming nominee (GSPR 15(7)).
- **Scheme-wise:** SCSS spouse-continuation **destroyed by multi-nomination** (spouse must be *sole* nominee); RD/TD/NSC/KVP continuation unregulated for joint nominees (engine default: closure unless a single taker).

## Repeal/savings notes

- Anything done under the repealed 1981/1960/1968 rules is deemed done under GSPR 2018 (Rule 26(2)); same savings in Act s.16(2).
- GSPR Rule 25: questions relating to periods **before** GSPR commencement are governed by the **POSB General Rules, 1981**. (Old rules therefore remain relevant for legacy disputes.)

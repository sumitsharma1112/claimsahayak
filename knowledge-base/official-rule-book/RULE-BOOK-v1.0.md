## 10. Known Limitations

1. **Provisional items excluded from the verified layer:** ₹50-fee abolition (G.S.R. 214(E)/SB 05/2025) and Internet-Banking nominee management (SB 04/2026) — official copies pending (M-1/M-2).
2. **POSB(CBS) Manual (corrected 31.12.2021)** not yet archived; operative Rule 60 text verified through the SB-Order chain instead (M-3/OQ-3).
3. **SB Order 31/2020** archived from a mirror of the signed order (officially-hosted copy pending, M-4); content corroborated by three officially-hosted instruments.
4. **Statutory discrepancy** Act s.4A(4) (3 months) vs GSPR 15(6) (6 months) — engine uses 6; legal review recommended (C-1/NV-RB-9).
5. **Regulatory gaps** (no official procedure): untraceable/unwilling co-nominee (OQ-14); freeze-release before settlement (OQ-28); authority column for heirs-of-nominee claims (OQ-11); RD/TD multi-claimant continuation (OQ-15 residue); simultaneous-death sequencing (excluded — general law).
6. **Harmonisation lags** in official texts: Form 11 footnotes and NSC/KVP internal clauses not yet updated for the 2023 GSPR amendment (C-3/C-12); SB 36/2020 field table pre-dates 488(E) (C-2).
7. NSI scheme consolidations verified to ~2021 with 2023 layered from SB Order 16/2023; a 2024–26 e-Gazette amendment sweep is pending (M-15).
8. Legacy-manual citations use archived-copy offsets, not print pages (V-8).
9. Hindi versions of instruments not systematically archived (English gazette texts controlled; SB Order 16/2023 carries both).

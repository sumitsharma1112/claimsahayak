# Official Forms Catalogue — Nomination & Deceased Claims

## A. GSPR 2018 statutory forms (Schedule I; verbatim texts in `sources/gspr-2018-gsr-1003e.pdf`)

| Form | Title (Schedule I) | Rule anchor | Use in deceased claims / nomination |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Form 1** (GSPR-1) | Application for opening an account | Rule 5 | ¶17 = **nomination section at opening**: up to 4 nominees with relationship, full address, **Aadhaar number of nominee**, DOB of minor nominee, **share of entitlement**, **nature of entitlement (trustee or owner)**; appointment of payee for minor nominee(s); **two witness signatures**; office block records nomination registration no./date |
| **Form 10** (GSPR-10) | Form for making nomination or change or cancellation of nomination | Rule 14 | Fresh/changed/cancelled nomination; same nominee table as Form 1; declaration whether it **supersedes previous nomination** (with old registration no./date) or is the first; passbook must be enclosed; **two witness blocks**; thumb impression of illiterate depositor attested (Rule 14(8): witnesses mandatory only for illiterate depositor); office registration serial |
| **Form 11** (GSPR-11) | Claim Form (Deceased) — "Application for settlement of an account of the deceased depositor by nominee or legal heirs" | Rule 15 | Enclosures checklist: (1) death certificate of depositor(s); (2) death certificate of deceased nominee(s) (***strike if N/A*); (3) succession certificate/LoA/attested probate copy (**strike if valid nomination exists*); (4) Letter of Indemnity(*); (5) Affidavit(*); (6) Letter of disclaimer on affidavit(*); (7) passbook/deposit receipt/statement. Footnote (*): required from **legal heirs, absent nomination, claims ≤ ₹5 lakh**. Second part "For office use only" = **sanction block** (SB Order 01/2023: SPM/PM sanctions here — no separate memo) + acquittance |
| **Form 12** (GSPR-12) | Letter of authorisation (physical infirmity/blindness) | Rule 11 | Operation through authorised literate individual (relevant when claimant is differently-abled) |
| **Form 13** (GSPR-13) | **Affidavit** | Rule 15 | Deponent(s) declare they are the **only heirs**; deceased died **intestate**; attested by **Oath Commissioner/Notary Public**. Per R60(4)(A) Note 2(ii): on **non-judicial stamp paper** per State Stamp Act |
| **Form 14** (GSPR-14) | **Letter of disclaimer** | Rule 15 | Other heirs **relinquish claims** in favour of claimant; deponents identified before **Oath Commissioner/Notary**; non-judicial stamp paper. Also used **among surviving nominees** to authorise one nominee to receive payment (R60(2)(xi) Note) |
| **Form 15** (GSPR-15) | **Bond of Indemnity** | Rule 15 | Claimant + **sureties** jointly & severally indemnify the Post Office for payment without LoA/succession certificate; **two witnesses**; attested by **Notary Public**; non-judicial stamp paper |
| **Form 16** (GSPR-16, ins. G.S.R. 488(E) 03.07.2023) | Certificate of non-availability of account opening form | Rule 13 | Transferor office certifies AOF untraceable; certificate includes **name of nominee(s)** — nomination particulars travel with transfers |

## B. DoP operational claim form (SB Order 31/2020 enclosure + Addendum 16.09.2020)

"Application for settlement of an account of the deceased depositor by nominee or legal heirs under National (Small) Savings Scheme" — the field version of Form 11 for POSB:

- Adds: claimant **mobile number**; option "**withdrawal of entire amount / transfer of the account/certificate(s) in my/our name**" (continuation);
- **Two witnesses** with name, address, mobile, signature — each with **self-attested ID & address proof attached** (physical presence not required per Addendum);
- "Witnesses accepted — Signature of Sr.PM/PM/SPM/**GDS BPM**";
- Claimant's self-attested ID + address proof mandatory;
- Sanction block (incl. Sanction Memo No. **only when** sanctioned by an administrative authority);
- Acquittance: cheque no. / credit to **PO Savings Account** or **Bank account (A/c no. + IFSC + first page of passbook/cancelled cheque)**; or transfer of account/certificate to claimant's name with **new AOF + Annexure-II KYC form + KYC documents**.

## C. Witness ID / address proof list (Annexure to Addendum dt. 16.09.2020; per KYC master circular SB Order 14/2012 + GSPR 2018)

Identity: Aadhaar, Passport, Driving Licence, Voter ID, Ration card with photo, PO identity card / Govt-PSU identity card (PPO, BPL card, MGNREGA job card signed by State Govt officer), certificate of address with photo from Govt-recognised educational institution (current students), PAN card.
Address: Aadhaar, Passport, Driving Licence, Voter ID, Ration card with address, photo ID with address (Central/State Govt/PSU), educational-institution certificate, salary slip of reputed employer with current address, National Population Register letter, utility bill ≤ 2 months (electricity/telephone/post-paid mobile/piped gas/water), property or municipal tax receipt, employer accommodation-allotment letter (Govt/statutory/PSU/scheduled bank/financial institution/listed company), **bank or post office passbook/statement with current address**.

## D. Ancillary official formats (SB Order 31/2020 enclosure)

- **Sanction Memo** (administrative offices only): valid **one year**; explicitly offers RD/TD/certificate holders liberty to defer payment and have the account/certificate **transferred to their name** till maturity.
- **Acknowledgment of Customer Request** (same-day issue; PO name, date/time, depositor, account, scheme, request serial no.).
- **Register of Deceased Claim Cases** (12 columns: case mark, receipt date, claimant particulars & relationship, deceased name, date of death, account/scheme, balance, date sent to higher authority, sanction particulars, payment particulars, remarks).
- **Reconciliation Certificate** (name-difference cases; issued by Divisional Head/Head of GPO/Gazetted HO).
